Features

Everything you need
to own your day.

Pie combines visual time blocking, habit tracking, and task management into one beautifully designed experience.

🕐

Clock-Based Planning

See your entire day as a pie chart on a 12-hour clock. Drag, block, and visualize your time at a glance.

🔥

Streak Tracking

Build consistency with daily streaks. Watch your fire grow as you complete tasks day after day.

📅

Calendar History

Review your past performance with a visual calendar. See completion rates and identify patterns.

📝

Encrypted Notes

Capture ideas securely with locally encrypted notes. Your thoughts stay private, always.

✅

Smart Checklists

Daily tasks and persistent goals in one view. Auto-detect missed tasks and stay accountable.
